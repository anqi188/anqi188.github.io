LaaS User Study

The layout of webpages could be optimized/rearranged, so that users can derive better experience regarding certain objectives. This user study aims at testing user performance with original and optimized webpages. Data is collected and used for scientific purposes only and strictly anonymized.


Test 1 Test 2 Test 3

Instruction:

Please read carefully before continuing

  1. This study will take about 10 minutes of your time. You will be presented with a series of webpages in the following trials. Getting yourself familiar with each objective item, then find and click it in the subsequent webpage as fast and accurately as possible.
  2. Before each trial, you will start with getting to know the objective item. Take your time to get familiar with them, click the 'Start the trial' button to continue the trial when you are ready. Noted that you are not supposed to check the objective item again once the trial is started.
  3. For each trial, timing starts right after you see the webpage, and stops after you click on the webpage. If the correct objective item is clicked, you will move on to the next trial. If not, you will see the red-cross and redo the trial.
  4. After finishing all trial, click the prompt button to download the study data (.txt and .csv files). Please send these 2 files to the contact person.
  5. By clicking the 'Start the test!' button you give your informed consent for the data collection (anonymized).
