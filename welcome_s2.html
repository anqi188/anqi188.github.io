LaaS User Study 2

LaaS is able to optimize and rearrage the layout of webpages, while the aesthetic quality might be affected. This study aims at collecting the perceived aesthetic quality for the design variants of selected webpage. Data is collected and used for scientific purposes only and strictly anonymized.
